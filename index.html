About

Venture Hacks is the first of multiple hackathons sponsored by the new Stevens Venture Center. The Venture Center aims to redefine entrepreneurial education and support rising student entrepreneurs. Venture Hacks will be 36 hours of talking about, building, and collaborating on ideas. While there is focus on technology, this hackathon centers on the creative process and entrepreneurship. Join the revolution in hackathons.

Mission

36 hours to better our community. Real people, real problems. Don't just hack: learn new technology, collaborate on a solution, and inspire not only those around you, but yourself.

Who

Venture Hacks aims to empower smart, passionate and motivated people. We provide a space to not only build something awesome, but to collaborate and work through problems with amazing people.

Incentives

We've got it all: cool prizes, tech, a good view, and of course, entrepreneurs. Come break in the brand new Venture Center while building your resume and a revolutionary project with your like-minded peers. You'll be exposed to Stevens alumns and entrepreneurship mentors.

What is a Hackathon?

Despite Hollywood's depiction of 'hackers' as those who steal millions from unsuspecting citizens or launch cyberattacks on governments, hackathons are actually productive (and not illegal!). Hackathons center on collaboration between computer programmers, designers, and other creative people to solve problems and prototype new ideas. While aggravated assault on Xerox machines is not unheard of, hackers at hackathons are helpful members of society.

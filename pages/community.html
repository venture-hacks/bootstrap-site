About

Venture Hacks is the first of multiple hackathons sponsored by the new Stevens Venture Center. Venture Hacks will be 36 hours of talking about, building, and collaborating on ideas. While there is focus on technology, this hackathon centers on the creative process and entrepreneurship. Join the revolution in hackathons.
